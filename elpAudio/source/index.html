elpAudio is a simple audio player using FMODex sound system.
Written in 100% pure Game Maker Language.
So small and simple -- you can even put it in your pocket.

elpAudio is a very customizable audio player.
It supports custom skins (they are called elpAudio themes), custom visualisers, plugins and such.

What audio types does elpAudio supports?

elpAudio with FMODex supports popular music formats, like:

  • MP3
  • OGG
  • WAV
  • FLAC
  • WV
  • XM
  • IT
  • MOD
  • S3M
  • ...and others...
    you can add your own supported music format with plugins
